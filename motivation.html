Self care

Self-care describes a conscious act one takes in order to promote their own physical, mental, and emotional health. There are many forms self-care may take. It could be ensuring you get enough sleep every night or stepping outside for a few minutes for some fresh air.

Meditation won't be effective if you're not gonna sleep a lot, you might end up falling asleep while doing it because you aren't taking good care of your body for it to get adequate rest >:(
Similarly, going to the gym once in a while won't relieve much stress if you're only constantly going to McDonald's and only going there because of the guilt. You need to take care of your basic needs ( eating healthily // having adequate sleep !! ) first if you want your stress relief activities to be effective.
