Silent Awareness

who we are

Hey there! We are a group of 11 Dunman Secondary School councillors who are deeply passionate about spreading awareness of mental health issues among students and normalising them in our society.

our goal

We hope that through this website, you guys can educate yourself on mental health and make it a more open subject.
We also hope that this will encourage more people to seek help // look for help and for you to know, you're not alone !

This way, we hope that you are more aware of your thoughts !
